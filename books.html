Surviving Christmas A Keegan Reese Mystery

Left with an aunt he barely knows, Keegan Rees is dreading an awkward Christmas. But, for all her chipperness and battleship playing, Keegan notices there’s something suspicious about his aunt. Cryptic phone calls, mysterious meetings in the mall, long absences. As time goes by, he stumbles upon more and more clues his aunt isn’t the crazy lady he always thought she was. In fact, he trips head first into her secret life and discovers it’ll take the two of them if they want to make it until Christmas. Find out the shocking discovery Keegan makes about his aunt right here!

Nora witch in training

It’s Halloween and Nora is facing her first ever beginners witch test. The dreaded Ms. White will be taking notes and watching her every move. Nora’s two friends happen to be monsters and the focus of a test section. But she and Ms. White have very different ideas of how they should be treated in the test, and as creatures altogether. Choosing between her heart and her future was not something Nora counted on. What will she choose? And how will it affect the lives of her and her friends? Find out what she chooses and what happens as a result right here.
